Who Am I?

Hotel Paradise is located in Jaipur’s lively city center, 2.4 mi from Jaipur Railway Station. It offers a rooftop swimming pool, free Wi-Fi and nonsmoking rooms with a 26-inch flat-screen TV. Modern décor and wooden flooring feature throughout the rooms at Paradise Hotel. Each well-appointed room is fitted with a personal safe and private bathroom with shower facilities. Guests can work out at the fitness center or rent a car to explore the city. Ticketing and travel arrangements can be made at the tour desk. Sunflower restaurant serves a variety of Mediterranean, Asian and India dishes. Light snacks and refreshing beverages can be enjoyed at Ice Bar. The Paradise is 2.5 mi from Jaipur Central Bus Stand and 9.9 mi from Jaipur International Airport. We speak your language!
